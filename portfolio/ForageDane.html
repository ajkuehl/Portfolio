Forage Dane: Explore the Parks of Dane County, Wisconsin

Forage Dane is a mobile responsive website that uses crowd sourced data, collected by fellow foragers, to create an introductory guide and interactive web map with the purpose to assist new foragers with identifying locations of foraging opportunities within Dane County, Wisconsin. The site additionally supplies new foragers the foundational guidelines needed to start foraging safely and ethically by providing resources of plant characteristics of commonly foraged items in Dane County, details on regulations of foraging within the county, health and wellbeing tips in regards to foraging safety.
